Cakeseable App

Responsive app and website

Cakeseable is a pastry app designed to make it easy for users to place orders and create custom cakes in a few simple steps.

UX design: digital wireframes, low-fidelity prototype, mockups, high-fidelity prototype, accessibility, responsive design.

The problem

Customers seeking a more convenient option than visiting the store.
Custumers desiring a cake that is more personalized than the store's options.

The goal

To allow Cakeseable users to place orders in a few simple steps and give them the option to customize cakes.

Understanding the user

User research. Resume

I interviewed users to understand their needs and design accordingly. A key group was seniors who struggled with online ordering and had mobility issues. While they confirmed some existing needs. The research also highlighted time constraints for custom cake orders.

Usability study. Findings

I conducted two rounds of usability testing. Findings from the first study informed the design evolution from paper and digital wireframes to mockups. The second study, conducted with a high-fidelity prototype, revealed areas for improvement in the mockups.

Study type:

Unmoderated usability study

Participants:

5 participants

Location:

Madrid, Spain (remote)

Length

30-50 minutes

Findings from the first round:

  1. Users who prefer to use the app as guests.
  2. Issues with removing products from the shopping cart.
  3. Too many steps in the checkout process.

Findings from the second round:

  1. Difficulty changing the language.
  2. Demand for a favorites feature.

Mockups

While the first designs offered some personalization, post-usability testing, I introduced additional features like unregistered access to the app.

Main user journey

Accessibility considerations

  1. Clear labels to facilitate screen reader interpretation of interactive elements.
  2. A main menu designed to guide users through the event search process more efficiently.

Takeways

Impact: The app allows users to quickly and easily access Santander's cultural programming, share their favorite activities, save events of interest, and be redirected to the official website to purchase tickets. As one user put it: “The app made it super easy to find a fun event for the weekend! I definitely recommend Cakeseable to everyone who wants to stay up-to-date on Santander's leisure activities”.

What I learned: While designing the Cakeseable app, I learned that initial ideas are just the starting point. Usability studies and user feedback influenced every version of the designs, and it's an ongoing process of improvement.
