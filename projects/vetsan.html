The problem

Some users do not keep up with reminders about their pets vaccinations and deworming and lack sufficient time for non-essential clinic visits.

The goal

The website design proposes a simple interface and a quick and comprehensive registration function so that the user can stay up-to-date on their pet's reminders. The goal is to increase user satisfaction with the clinic's services and ensure that their pet's veterinary treatments are up-to-date.

Understanding the user

User research. Resume

I carried out interviews and developed empathy maps to grasp user needs and design accordingly. A key user group identified in the research were young adults with multiple responsibilities, frustrated by the challenge of staying on top of their pet's vaccinations. Another group of users with busy schedules wanted to minimize unnecessary clinic visits, preferring to stay informed about their pet's health and manage reminders conveniently from their phones.

A competitive product audit has given me valuable insights into the strengths and weaknesses of the app and web platform, guiding the future development efforts.

Opportunities

Takeways

Impact: The application allows users to quickly and conveniently access their pet's reminders, providing them with real-time relevant information without having to visit the clinic or call to verify upcoming vaccinations and deworming. A user testimonial: "The online registration feature is exactly what I needed! It allows me to access reminders quickly and schedule a vaccination appointment for my pet in advance."

What I learned: User interviews are essential for creating a design tailored to their lifestyle, tastes, hobbies, concerns... Knowing the user well ensures that the design perfectly matches their needs.
