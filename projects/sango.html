SanGo

Responsive app and website

SanGo is a platform designed to help people discover and enjoy cultural events in Santander city. It provides detailed information about events and allows users to filter by category for a personalized experience. It boasts a responsive web design that prioritizes accessibility.

The problem

The scarcity of information and the narrow range of events advertised in traditional media creates a barrier for some users.

The goal

To offer a user-friendly tool that enables everyone to stay informed about cultural events in Santander.

Understanding the user

User research. Resume

Through user interviews and empathy mapping, I sought to understand the needs of the target audience. A significant segment identified in the research was older adults with a high cultural background who expressed frustration at the challenges they faced in finding information about upcoming literary events. The findings corroborated my initial hypotheses regarding certain user needs, but they also unveiled a more nuanced picture. A substantial proportion of interviewees indicated a strong desire to attend a wider variety of cultural events than those advertised in traditional media, which many found to be unappealing.

Persona

Competitive audit

A competitive product audit has given me valuable insights into the strengths and weaknesses of the app and web platform, guiding the future development efforts.

Gaps

  1. User experience: The interface could benefit from a redesign to improve usability and provide a more intuitive experience.
  2. Limited interactivity.
  3. Focus on cultural events.
  4. Few accessibility tools.
  5. Complex user experience.
  6. Generalized approach.

Opportunities

  1. Customize the user interface to reflect the local culture and specific aesthetic preferences of Santander.
  2. Enhance the user experience through more intuitive and user-friendly navigation.
  3. Provide accessibility tools.
  4. Conduct regular usability tests and gather feedback to continuously improve the platform.
  5. Leverage advanced event management tools, such as ticket management, personalized reminders, and relevant user notifications.

Starting the design

Digital wireframes

I conducted a rapid ideation session to address the issues highlighted in the competitive analysis. My primary focus was on improving the visibility and discoverability of events within the app. Once I had some rough sketches on paper, I started designing the app. The main goal was to make it easy for users to browse events by category.

Takeways

Impact: The app allows users to quickly and easily access Santander's cultural programming, share their favorite activities, save events of interest, and be redirected to the official website to purchase tickets. As one user put it: “The app made it super easy to find a fun event for the weekend! I definitely recommend SanGo to everyone who wants to stay up-to-date on Santander's leisure activities”.

What I learned: While designing the SanGo app, I learned that initial ideas are just the starting point. Usability studies and user feedback influenced every version of the designs, and it's an ongoing process of improvement.
